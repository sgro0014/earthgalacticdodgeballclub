Home of the Blue Ocean Warriors

The Blue Ocean Warriors have a proud history in Galactic Dodgeball. We are accepting of all aliens. Our advantage in number of fingers allow us to perform at a higher level than the rest of the Milkyway.

  • Home is Earth

    Spanning 7 continents and 196 Countries, the Earth Galactic Dodgeball Team plays in all milkyway galaxy tournaments, from juniors to the highest level.

  • #1 Champions

    With 126 championships, the most of any galactic dodgeball club, the Blue Ocean Warriors are the greatest to ever play the game due to our planet conditions.

  • Backed from the Best

    With flights from Virgin Galactic, Nike for clothing and Red Bull for fuel, our teams are provided a celebrity lifestyle no matter the league they are in.

  • Leaders of Education

    From the dawn of homosapiens, we have realised that education is the one of the most important things. Therefore, our academey provides extensive galactic dodgeball training.
